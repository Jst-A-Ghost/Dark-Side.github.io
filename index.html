About Me

Welcome to Psychotic Poems, a collection of verses that reflect the chaotic beauty of life and the depth of emotions. Here, you'll find whispers of the night, echoes of dawn, and melodies carried by the breeze. Each poem is a piece of my soul, laid bare for you to experience and connect with.

Whispers of the Night

The night whispers softly to the soul,
As shadows dance and stories unfold.
Beneath the stars, dreams take flight,
In the quiet calm of the velvet night.

Echoes of Dawn

With dawn's first light, hope is reborn,
As golden rays caress the morn.
The world awakens, fresh and new,
Bathed in the morning's tender hue.

A Whispered Breeze

The breeze whispers secrets to the trees,
Carried gently on the seas.
A melody sung by nature's choir,
Filling hearts with soft desire.
